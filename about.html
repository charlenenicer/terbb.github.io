Hello there,

I'm Trevin Wong (terb), and I'm a 3rd year Computer Science student currently attending UBC. I enjoy making and playing video games!

Unlike a lot of programmers, I never got into programming too much as a kid. I always thought "why program games when you can play them?"

However, ever since starting school, it's funny how I've slowly found myself on the other side. Instead of playing games, I mostly spend my time creating them. I think it's because as a gamer, having played a lot, I've always thought "what if something like this existed that I could play?"

And now that I have the ability to create those kinds of games, it's been really rewarding and addicting to do so. I hope you enjoy playing my games too.
